Work

I'm a UX/UI designer working at the intersection of product design, data viz and AI

I design meaningful digital experiences, by working from UX research and product strategy to UX/UI design and working with code to conceptualize ideas

My process is driven by understanding users needs, drawing from both quantitative and qualitative research methods to create a fuller picture of the problem. From ethnographic studies to processing data to find hidden patterns and insights. I combine my technical background with human-centered approach to design novel solutions to complex problems.

I currently work as a consultant helping startups and think-thanks to develop new products, technologies and services at the intersection of data visualization, HCI, data science, machine learning, and blockchain. Prior I was a design lead at Samsung Mobile Lab, and Globo.com and helped bring many products to life, reaching millions worldwide.
