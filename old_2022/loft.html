Loft

Mobile TV and connected house hub

Loft was a mobile tablet concept designed by the team at UXCA (San Francisco, CA), and released by Samsung as Galaxy View. The original concept sought to occupy the space between a large tablet, a portable smart TV, and a connected house hub. My role in the project consisted of designing, and prototyping the main interaction model, and the sound UI, which featured a digital assistant, and distinctive sounds for different device notifications.

Discipline: Connected house, product development
Role: Sound UI, interaction design
Team: 10+ designers, engineers, prototyping, generative sound
Year: 2015
