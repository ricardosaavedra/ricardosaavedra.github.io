About
Work

I’m a UX designer working at the intersection of interaction design, data visualization, machine learning and data science

I’m a UX designer with over 10 years of experience creating end-to-end products. My practice involves observing behaviors in people and systems, to making hypothesis about design approaches, and testing them iteratively through low-to-high fidelity prototypes. My background in computer science combined with human-centered design methods allows me to explore design solutions that are both innovative and delightful to use. Over the last 5 years, I’ve been focused on designing experiences at the intersection of data visualization, machine learning and data science.

As a design consultant, I help startups and think-thanks to develop new products, technologies and services working from UX Research and Design Strategy to UX/UI design. Prior, I was a design lead at Samsung Mobile Lab, and Globo.com and helped bring many products to life, reaching millions of users worldwide.

I bring 10 years of experience in reserach and development in the space of:

Mobile-centric Applications

Data Visualization and Analytics

Interactive Machine Learning Systems

Wearables & Hardware

Physical Computing

IoT and Automation

Computer Vision & Sensing

Blockchain-based Products

Method and Skills

Strategy & Leadership

I try to balance the business goals of the organization against the limits of technology and users' needs. I've developed the leadership skills and storytelling ability necessary to communicate the value of design to multiple stakeholders in cross-functional teams.

Visual Design

I use color, typography, icons, information design, and visual hierarchy to clarify data, and indicate relationships between elements. I strive to achieve the ideal balance between familiar visual motifs and unexpected aesthetics to trigger curiosity and interest from users.

User Centered

While my work is informed by technical and business requirements, I put users at the center of everything I do. By focusing on understanding people and their social environment, design can play a strategic role in shaping how product and services evolve to meet user needs.

Working With Data

I’ve developed a unique process that combines ethnography and data science to embrace a more complex understanding of the problem domain and user needs. I see qualitative and quantitative research methods as complementary tools to arrive at new valuable insights.
