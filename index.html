Selected Work

Magic Room

Meta/Reality Labs

Magic Rooms enables distributed teams to be in the same room with each other in mixed reality increasing the sense of presence. I was responsible for UX design across the end-to-end meeting experience including room scan, onboarding, and a wide range of UX/Visual improvements. I also led the development of new UX patterns for VR and MR, increasing user adoption in immersive and mixed-reality environments. I worked closely with engineers across many product and HCI challenges to bring the original project prototype to a delightful user experience. Magic Room was featured by MZ at Connect in 2022 as "the future of meetings in augmented reality".

Horizon Workrooms

Meta/Reality Labs

I was responsible for leading the design, and development of new interaction patterns in mixed reality aimed at reducing friction across social (meetings in VR/MR) and solo productivity use cases (extended computer user in VR/MR). I collaborated with UX researchers to better understand user needs, and worked closely with designers and engineers to prototype and validate design solutions. My work impacted short-term releases and contributed to future product redesigns and new hardware development.

Wonder

As the principal designer and part of the founding team, I led many different product design initiatives spanning user research, growth, branding, and product strategy. I worked closely with the researchers to understand user needs to inform core product/strategic decisions and new product developments. My contributions allowed the company to successfully innovate and reach product and design maturity by reaching 1M monthly users in 15 months.

Samsung OS redesign

Samsung Mobile Lab

At Samsung Mobile Lab, was part of a cross-disciplinary design team tasked to redesign Samsung's OS (TouchWiz). I worked with HCI researchers to map out pain points and user stories to establish a design direction for the team (IA, Visual, Motion). I was also responsible for conceptualizing and designing key signature interactions such as task-switcher, mode selection, and external device control. I worked across the end-to-end design process, collaborating with motion and visual designers to execute and iterate on final designs and prototypes. The output of my work allowed the team to establish a unique design language for Samsung, having a direct impact on today's Samsung's OS design strategy

Gradient

Serpentine Galleries

Serpentine Gallery R&D commissioned me to envision a new model for collaboration with machine learning algorithms. I worked alongside a group of composers to design early prototypes and concepts. Following project ideation, I developed the software and interface, validated the solution, and presented my learnings in a panel hosted by Serpentine. The project's conclusion validated the hypothesis that it is viable and desirable to build interfaces that allow users to guide complex algorithms for artistic production meaningfully.

Samsung Wearable
Camera

Samsung Mobile Lab

At Samsung Mobile Lab, I was responsible for the original concept and development of a new wearable camera for Samsung. Following research and ideation, I led a team of cross-functional designers to iterate on industrial design, hardware, and software specifications to enable validation with users. Samsung HQ considered releasing the product concept, but hardware costs challenged the project viability at that time.

Samsung Smart
Camera

Samsung Mobile Lab

I led a team of designers and researchers to develop new features and products in the space of new image capture devices (hardware and software). I was responsible for the original auto-capture concept and worked with engineers to develop the unique software design that powered the auto-capture/reels generation mechanism. While developing the concept, I created four unique IPs for Samsung, most notably the Auto Capture feature found on most iPhone cameras today.

Galaxy Tab

Samsung Mobile Lab

I contributed to UX design for the original concept that led to the release of Galaxy Tab. I worked with a cross-disciplinary team of industrial and visual designers to turn user insights into a tangible product proposition. Following the concept presentation, the prototype evolved into Galaxy Tab, selling millions of units worldwide.
